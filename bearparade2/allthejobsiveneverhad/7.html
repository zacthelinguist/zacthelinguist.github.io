GROUNDSKEEPER
 


I could keep so much ground. Like, sand and dirt and soil. Keep it in jars. Store the jars in a giant warehouse. Create tiny eco-systems in the jars and write essays on the ecosystems, like, "The mites in Jar A34 are procreating at a rapid pace!" I applied for a groundskeeper position recently and they asked me if I knew how to operate a riding lawn mower. I asked why, and the guy said "to mow the yard" like I was some kind of idiot. He's the idiot, though. Why would you break the ground? You're supposed to be keeping it!
